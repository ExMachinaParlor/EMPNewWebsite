About Us


At the Ex Machina Parlor, we welcome individuals from all backgrounds who have a passion for technology, whether you’re a seasoned hacker, a computer science enthusiast, or simply someone curious about cybersecurity and emerging tech.

We are a dynamic community focused on advancing knowledge in technology, cybersecurity, and the future of the internet. We provide a platform for learning, collaboration, and innovation, adhering to a strong ethical framework. While we support technological exploration, we emphasize that all activities must align with legal and ethical standards.

Note: We do not endorse illegal activities and are not responsible for the misuse of any information or training shared through our platform.

Our Projects






Explore our cutting-edge cybersecurity robotics projects, designed for practical security applications in both offensive and defensive operations.



Tengu Marauder

The Tengu Marauder is an advanced mobile platform built on the ESP32 microcontroller, designed to test WiFi network security. It combines robust robotic control with video streaming capabilities, making it a versatile tool for network security assessments. Key features include:

Feature Description
WiFi Scanning Perform reconnaissance and identify network vulnerabilities.
Deauthentication Attacks Test the resilience of WiFi networks against targeted deauthentication.
Packet Sniffing Capture and analyze network traffic for security auditing.
Robotic Movement with ROS2 ROS2 enables precise movement control and autonomous navigation.
Live Video Streaming Integrated ESP32-CAM provides real-time video feed for situational awareness.



Learn more about the Tengu Marauder at DEFCON 32.



Strix Interceptor

The Strix Interceptor is an open-source UAV developed for drone interception and defense against RF-based attacks. It offers autonomous flight capabilities and RF spoofing, making it a powerful tool for securing airspace from rogue drones or conducting research in RF defense. Key components include:

Component Description
Pixhawk Flight Controller Provides stability control and autonomous flight.
Raspberry Pi Integration Enables data processing and custom programming for enhanced capabilities.
HackRF One Allows for RF spoofing and testing of RF-based security systems.




Learn more about the Strix Interceptor at DEFCON 31.
